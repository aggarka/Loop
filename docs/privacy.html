Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Loop app ("Loop", "we", "us") handles your information. We built Loop to be privacy-first: your networking data stays on your device and syncs privately to your own account.

Information you provide

Loop stores the information you choose to enter, including:

  • People you add (name, company, title, email, phone, tags, and how they were added).
  • Interactions you log (date, type, notes, outcomes, and follow-up dates).
  • Optional data from features you use — business-card scans (processed on your device) and contacts you choose to import.

How your data is stored and synced

Loop is local-first: your data is stored on your device and works offline. When you sign in, your data syncs to your private account on our backend (hosted on Supabase) so it stays consistent across your devices. Access is protected so that only you can read or write your own records.

Authentication

You sign in with Sign in with Apple, Google, or email. We receive a user identifier and, depending on the provider and your choices, an email address, used solely to create and secure your account. Loop does not post anything to these services.

AI features and PII protection

When you ask Loop to summarize notes, extract action items, or draft a follow-up, the relevant text is processed by an AI model through our backend. Before any text leaves your device, personally identifiable information — names, company names, email addresses, and phone numbers — is replaced with neutral placeholders on your device. The AI provider receives only this redacted text and never the real identities of your contacts. The AI endpoint is configured for no data retention and is not used to train models. AI features are optional.

Device permissions

  • Camera — only when you scan a business card; images are processed on your device.
  • Contacts — only when you import or export contacts, at your request.
  • Notifications — only to remind you of follow-ups you schedule.

Loop works without granting these permissions; the related features are simply unavailable.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not include third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings. Deleting your account permanently removes your account and all associated people and interactions from our backend, and clears the data stored locally on your device.

Children

Loop is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
