What Loop does

Loop is a personal networking companion for iPhone and iPad. Log a conversation right after it happens, capture what you committed to, and let Loop surface clear, timely follow-ups so relationships never fall through the cracks.

  • People & interactions — keep a private record of everyone you meet and every conversation.
  • Next Actions — a single feed of overdue and upcoming follow-ups.
  • Reminders — local notifications so you never miss a follow-up.
  • AI assistance — summarize notes, extract action items, and draft follow-ups, with names and contact details removed on your device before any AI processing.
  • Business card scanning & contacts import — add people in seconds.
  • Private sync — your data syncs across your devices and is only ever visible to you.

Privacy-first by design

Loop stores your data on your device and syncs it privately to your account. Before notes are sent for AI processing, personal details (names, companies, emails, phone numbers) are replaced with placeholders on your device — so the AI service never sees who your contacts are.
